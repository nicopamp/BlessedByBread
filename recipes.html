Blueberry French Toast

Ingredients

Sourdough Sandwich Bread (4 slices, 1 inch thick)

  • 2 eggs
  • ½ cup whole milk
  • ½ tsp pure vanilla
  • Butter
  • 1 cup fresh blueberries
  • Pure maple syrup

Instructions

  • Whisk together the eggs, milk, and vanilla.
  • Heat a skillet to medium-high and coat with butter.
  • Dip sliced bread into egg mixture, turning to saturate fully.
  • Cook on both sides until golden brown.
  • Top with blueberries and maple syrup.
